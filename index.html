What is Heart Disease?

Heart disease includes various conditions that affect heart function, the most common being coronary artery disease.

Did You Know?

  • Heart disease is a leading cause of death in adults over 45 in the UK.
  • Many cases are preventable with lifestyle changes.

Symptoms:

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue, dizziness, or nausea

Risk Factors

Understand your risks to make informed choices:

  • Lifestyle Factors: Diet, inactivity, smoking, alcohol
  • Age: Higher risk over 45
  • Family History: Increased risk with family history
  • High Blood Pressure & Cholesterol
  • Diabetes

Steps for a Healthier Heart

Practical lifestyle changes can make a big difference:

  • Eat a Balanced Diet: More fruits, vegetables, whole grains
  • Exercise Regularly: Aim for 30 minutes most days
  • Quit Smoking: Reduces heart disease risk significantly
  • Limit Alcohol: Stick to recommended guidelines
  • Manage Stress: Try relaxation techniques daily
  • Monitor Your Health: Regular check-ups are key
